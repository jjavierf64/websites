About Me

I am an Engineering Physicist specialized in quantum computing, high-performance computing, and data science. I am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Quantum Science and Technology at the University of Barcelona, focusing on quantum computation, quantum information theory, algorithms, and experimental techniques.

My work spans software development, applied cryptography, and algorithmic optimization in industrial R&D environments. I enjoy building efficient and scalable computational tools and collaborating with multidisciplinary teams to solve technical problems with clear practical impact.

Beyond research and engineering, I founded Quantum Learning Wave, an independent educational initiative that designs workshops and open-source material on quantum computing and engineering physics. I am motivated by scientific curiosity, rigorous thinking, and the possibility of contributing to emerging quantum technologies.
